**KEY SENTENCE** 🔍:

> "Experts and advocacy groups argue that access to education must be considered a fundamental human right that cannot be suspended even during armed conflict."

[ 構文分析 ]
S: Experts and advocacy groups / V: argue / that節 (名詞節・目的語)
that節内: S: access to education / V: must be considered / C: a fundamental human right
関係代名詞節: that cannot be suspended even during armed conflict（先行詞 a fundamental human right を修飾）
副詞句: even during armed conflict（武力紛争中でさえ）

[ 入試ポイント ]
「argue that S + V」（〜と主張する）は意見・主張を導く頻出表現で和訳問題の定番。「must be considered + 名詞」は受動態+助動詞の構造で、「〜とみなされなければならない」という義務の意味を持つ。関係代名詞 that が先行詞 a fundamental human right を修飾する構造の把握は内容一致問題で問われる。「even during armed conflict」の even の強調用法も頻出。
